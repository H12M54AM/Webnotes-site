Welcome, to WebNotes
Webnotes is an offline tool which saves your notes locally. it allows you to create keepsakes for your notes and sorts it immidieately to your chosen keepsake. This gives full control for you to govern the information on your device. WebNotes is also open source, which brings more creativity and imagination for when a user can customize their UI or UX to their personal preferences.
WebNotes can be install on all mainstream OS's, such as MacOS, Linux and Windows. More OS's will be avaliable and only Ubuntu, Windows 10 and 11 are avaliable. MacOS supported updates range from Mojave to Monteray
